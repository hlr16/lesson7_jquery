Eclair Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 box of graham crackers
  • 2 pkg French Vanilla Instant Pudding
  • 3 c cold milk
  • 8 oz cool whip
  • 2 pkg Nestles choco bake
  • 1 1/2 c powdered sugar
  • 2 T white Karo Syrup
  • 2 t Vanilla
  • 3 T cold milk

Instructions

Butter 13"x9" pan to prepare filling beat well the pudding and milk.

Add col whip and beat again.

Put layer of graham crackers on bottom of pan

Add 1/2 filling and spread.

Add another layer of graham crackers and remainder of filling.

Top another layer of graham crackers and frosting.

For frosting beat choco syrup, sugar, vanilla and milk.
